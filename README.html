Declaration of an aimed way of life

This is my waycast. I use it to declare my final goal, my choice of means and my reasons for the two — what I call an ‘aimed way of life’. In other words, I use the waycast to answer these questions:

  • Where am I going?
  • Why?
  • What steps will take me there?
  • Why do I think so?

And eventually, as the waycast and tools are further developed:

  • What step am I on?
  • Who am I working with?
  • How does my way relate to others?

Ultimately I hope the waycast proves a good framework for developing further tools. Some of these I would base on earlier prototypes, especially recombinant text and primary cascades — one to enable collaborative way formation and the other way-guided electoral coordination.
