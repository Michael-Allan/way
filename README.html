My waycast, declaring my way

This is my waycast. Through it I declare my final goal and the chosen means of achieving it. In other words, I use the waycast to answer these questions:

  • Where am I going?
  • Why?
  • What steps will take me there?

And, when the waycast and tools are further developed:

  • What step am I on?
  • Who am I working with?
  • How does my way relate to others?
