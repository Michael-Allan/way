Normative ethic

These files describe a normative ethic grounded on the value of an on-going existence for humanity, and elaborated through an entailed commitment to the conditions of reason.

While the files may be read in any order, they were written with a particular order in mind:

  1. Eternity by way of reason
  2. Structural conditions
  3. Pillars of reason
  4. Right and wrong
  5. Rights
