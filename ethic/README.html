The default morals of a rational nature at risk

These files develop a normative ethic from a ground in moral uncertainty through an entailed care for the enabling conditions of rational action.

While the files may be read in any order, they were written with a particular order in mind:

  1. Moral uncertainty binds
  2. Nightfall bares the prospect
  3. The bounden search for certainty
  4. Precarious conditions
  5. Right and wrong
  6. Rights
